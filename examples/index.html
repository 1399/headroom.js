Headroom.js

Give your pages some headroom. Hide your header until you need it.

Headroom playground

This is a playground where you can tinker with headroom's options and see what effect they have. The example styles are using slightly tweaked effects from the fantastic animate.css.

As you change the options they are auotomatically applied to the page's header. Example code is also generated as you make changes.
